About me

I am engaged in scientific research in the Spatio-Temporal Social Sensing Lab ($S^3$-Lab), supervised by Prof. Zhou Huang. Before that, I received my B.S. Degree on GIScience at Peking University in 2020.

My research interests are in Intelligent Transportation, Network Analysis, Spatio-Temporal Data Mining and GeoAI.
